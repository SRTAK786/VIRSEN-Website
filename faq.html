FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VIRSEN ecosystem.

What is VIRSEN?
VIRSEN is a next-generation blockchain ecosystem focused on transparency, innovation, and long-term sustainability. Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), it connects staking, rewards, and future Web3 services into one growing ecosystem.
How to buy VNST tokens?
You can buy VNST tokens from PancakeSwap or through our partner exchanges (coming soon). Connect your wallet and swap BNB or USDT for VNST. Always verify the contract address.
What is the staking APY?
Staking APY ranges from 12% to 28% depending on the pool and lock period. You earn 2X rewards in VNT for staking your VNST tokens. Flexible staking is also available.
Is VIRSEN audited?
Yes, our smart contracts are built with industry best practices and security standards. We prioritize security and transparency. Full audit reports will be published.
What is the 5-level referral system?
You earn referral rewards across 5 levels: 5% (Level 1), 3% (Level 2), 2% (Level 3), 1% (Level 4), and 1% (Level 5) on your downline's staking rewards. Build your network and earn.
What is the total supply of VNST?
The total supply of VNST is 100,000,000. 100% community owned with no tokens reserved for the company at launch. All tokens are in circulation.
What is the total supply of VNT?
The total supply of VNT is 210,000,000. VNT is used for staking rewards and is distributed to users based on staking and ecosystem activity.
What is the 90 days free claim (VNTT)?
VNTT is a claim token that allows users to claim daily rewards for 90 days absolutely free. It's designed to increase community engagement, onboard new users, and expand the ecosystem.
What wallets are supported?
We support MetaMask, TrustWallet, and WalletConnect. You can connect any BSC-compatible wallet to stake and claim rewards.
How does the buy-back mechanism work?
The company uses 5% withdrawal fee to buy back reward tokens from the market, which helps maintain token value and ensures ecosystem sustainability. All transactions are transparent.
When is the public launch?
The public launch is scheduled for 2025 with a DEX listing. Follow our social channels for exact dates and announcements. Private sale is currently open.
What is the fixed buy & sell price?
VNST has a fixed price of 0.10 USDT. This ensures no price manipulation and fair trading for everyone in the community.
How can I join the team?
We are actively hiring! Check our careers page or reach out via Telegram. We're looking for developers, marketers, and community managers.
Is there a lock-up period for staking?
Staking pools have flexible and locked options. Flexible staking has lower APY, while locked staking (30, 90, 180 days) offers higher rewards.
What are the future utilities of VNTT?
VNTT will be integrated into multiple features including NFT marketplace, gaming ecosystem, and upcoming utilities. The total supply will be announced soon.
Where can I find the whitepaper?
You can download the VIRSEN Whitepaper 2026 from the whitepaper page. It's available in PDF format and covers all aspects of the ecosystem.
What is the company revenue model?
The company earns a 5% withdrawal fee on every reward withdrawal. This fee is used for buy-back of reward tokens, which supports ecosystem sustainability.
Is VIRSEN built on Binance Smart Chain?
Yes, VIRSEN is built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) for fast transactions, low fees, and wide adoption. BSC provides a secure and scalable foundation.
How do I withdraw my staking rewards?
You can withdraw your VNT rewards anytime from the staking dashboard. A 5% fee is applied, and the remaining 95% goes directly to your wallet.
What makes VIRSEN different from other projects?
VIRSEN is 100% community owned with no hidden supply. We offer real utility, 2X rewards, 5-level referral system, 90 days free claim, and complete transparency on-chain.
