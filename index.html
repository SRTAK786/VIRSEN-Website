FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VIRSEN ecosystem.

What is VIRSEN?
VIRSEN is a next-generation blockchain ecosystem focused on transparency, innovation, and long-term sustainability. Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), it connects staking, rewards, and future Web3 services.
How to buy VNST tokens?
You can buy VNST tokens from PancakeSwap or through our partner exchanges (coming soon). Connect your wallet and swap BNB or USDT for VNST.
What is the staking APY?
Staking APY ranges from 12% to 28% depending on the pool and lock period. You earn 2X rewards in VNT for staking your VNST tokens.
Is VIRSEN audited?
Yes, our smart contracts are built with industry best practices and security standards. We prioritize security and transparency.
What is the 5-level referral system?
You earn referral rewards across 5 levels: 5% (Level 1), 3% (Level 2), 2% (Level 3), 1% (Level 4), and 1% (Level 5) on your downline's staking rewards.
What is the total supply of VNST?
The total supply of VNST is 100,000,000. 100% community owned with no tokens reserved for the company at launch.
When is the public launch?
The public launch is scheduled for 2025 with a DEX listing. Follow our social channels for exact dates and announcements.
What is the 90 days free claim (VNTT)?
VNTT is a claim token that allows users to claim daily rewards for 90 days absolutely free. It's designed to increase community engagement and onboard new users.
What wallets are supported?
We support MetaMask, TrustWallet, and WalletConnect. You can connect any BSC-compatible wallet.
How does the buy-back mechanism work?
The company uses 5% withdrawal fee to buy back reward tokens from the market, which helps maintain token value and ensures ecosystem sustainability.
